How to Care For Your Guinea Pig

Guinea pigs require a lot more care than you might think. You need to feed them twice a day, refill their water, and make sure you give them lots of love and affection, otherwise they might die of depression or lack of care. Since guinea pigs only live for around 5-7 years, you might as well give themthe best life possible. "But how?" You might ask. Well don't worry, I've got you covered. Here you will learn how to properly take care of your guinea pig and make their life worth living.
